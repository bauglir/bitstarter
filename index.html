We have all been there, from classes to business meetings, at some point or another the amount of information one has to take in just becomes too much and we simply doze off. This causes us to sometimes miss important information, making us regret later on that we did not pay enough attention.

This is where oBB comes into play. It allows you to either specify a set of your own buzzwords, or randomly pick a set of predefined words, to build a bingo game you can play with others in real-time over the Internet. The competitive edge provided by the bingo game is often enough to liven up even the dullest presentation, making sure you don't miss any important information.

The application is completely web-based and uses the latest technologies to provide a smooth, cross-platform, real-time experience. The game will run as smooth on your laptop computer as it will on any smartphone platform, allowing for discreet games during somewhat more sensitive meetings. Although winners can of course be loudly celebrated, the game makes the winner known as soon as any player manages to get a full row of buzzwords on his or her card.

The game is currently scheduled for a December 2013 release, pledge now for early access!

The main cost driver for this project are the hosting costs. Although the game will be free-to-play, the free tier will only allow a maximum of 5 players per game and will lack the ability to use custom word lists. Backers will receive 3 custom word lists for every $5 pledged and their hosted games will allow up to 15 simultaneous players. Backers who pledge more than $25 will get the ability to pick larger boards. Backers who pledge more than $50 will get access to different win conditions as well (instead of just x-in-a-row).

Pledge now to give your (or other people's) presentations that little bit extra to keep your audience engaged!

FAQ

Do I have to pay to participate in a bingo game?
No, there will a free tier for hosting limited functionality bingo games and any game can be joined through an invite of the organizer.
Will the different tier rewards be available after the crowdfunder closes?
Yes, the custom word lists, larger per-board player base, different board sizes and custom win conditions will be available at a later time as well. Most likely through a subscription service or through one-off micropayments
Will the games support more than 15 players per game?
Additional player slots will most likely be purchasable at a later date
